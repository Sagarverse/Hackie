# 📱 HACKIE PRO: THE SIMPLE GUIDE

Welcome to **Hackie Pro**. This is your all-in-one digital toolkit for checking how safe your devices, websites, and connections really are. Think of it as a "Swiss Army Knife" for your phone that uses advanced AI (like a smart robot assistant) to help you find security gaps.

---

## 🕵️ HOW TO HIDE HACKIE (STEALTH MODE)
If you don't want anyone to know you have this app, you can turn on **Stealth Mode**.
*   **What it does**: It changes the app's icon and name to look like a normal **Calculator**.
*   **How to get back**: Open the "Calculator," type **`1337`**, and press **`=`**. The real app will pop open!
*   **Extra Safety**: You can also set it to ask for your **Fingerprint** or **Face ID** every time you open a tool.

## 🚀 THE MAIN TOOLS (IN PLAIN ENGLISH)

### 1. The App Tester (Neural QA)
*   **What it does**: It automatically clicks through an app to see if it breaks or has bugs.
*   **Use it for**: Checking if a new app you downloaded is safe or stable.

### 2. The Website Scanner (Web Auditor)
*   **What it does**: It scans a website to find "open doors" that hackers might use.
*   **Use it for**: Making sure your own website or blog is locked down tight.

### 3. The Script Writer (Payload Forge)
*   **What it does**: It uses AI to write special computer instructions (scripts) for you.
*   **Use it for**: Automating tasks on your computer or testing how it reacts to certain commands.

### 4. The Wi-Fi Expert (Rogue Horizon)
*   **What it does**: It lets you see all the Wi-Fi signals around you and test if they are easy to break into.
*   **Use it for**: Checking if your home Wi-Fi password is strong enough.

### 5. The Ghost Sensor (Sensor Lab)
*   **What it does**: It uses your phone's built-in sensors to find "invisible" signals.
*   **Use it for**: Finding hidden electronic gadgets or seeing if a room has weird electrical spikes.

### 6. The People Search (OSINT Ghost)
*   **What it does**: It searches the internet to find public information about a person or company.
*   **Use it for**: Seeing what kind of your own information is floating around the web for anyone to find.

### 7. The Bluetooth Trickster (Bluetooth Shadow)
*   **What it does**: It finds all nearby Bluetooth devices and can even "spam" them with funny (or annoying) popups.
*   **Use it for**: Testing how your devices handle lots of connection requests at once.

### 8. The Device Mimic (Mirror Lab)
*   **What it does**: It makes your phone pretend to be a trusted device, like your own Bluetooth speaker.
*   **Use it for**: Testing if your computer is too "trusting" when it sees a familiar device name.
